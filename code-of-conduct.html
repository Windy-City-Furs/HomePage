Code of Conduct

Our goal is to create an inclusive, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone, regardless of background. By participating, you agree to uphold these standards.

Expected Behavior

  • Participate actively and authentically.
  • Respect others in speech and action.
  • Collaborate before escalating conflict.
  • Avoid demeaning or discriminatory behavior.
  • Be mindful of surroundings; report issues.
  • Respect venues and all their patrons.

Unacceptable Behavior

  • Violence or threats against others.
  • Discriminatory jokes or language.
  • Displaying explicit or violent material.
  • Doxing or sharing personal data.
  • Personal insults or harassment.
  • Inappropriate recording or photography.
  • Unwelcome physical contact or advances.
  • Stalking, intimidation, or disruption.
  • Zoophile or nazi references: immediate ban.
  • Overconsumption of alcohol or substances.

Weapons Policy

No weapons, real or replica, are allowed. Violations will result in immediate removal.

Consequences

Violations may result in warning, suspension, or permanent ban without refund.

Reporting

Please report any concerns to an event organizer immediately. Your safety matters.

Grievances

If you believe you were unfairly accused, contact a group admin with your concerns.
